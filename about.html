A local ma and pa shop located in Southern California California, Fortune Bakery has transformed into an icon of the San Gabriel Valley. Since 1985, we have served our community through affordable, yet delicious baked goods. We carry cakes, cupcakes, cookies and even sugar-free treats. Each cake is unique, made with love and care at our location. Because of the value we place on our customers and their experience with us, we are honored that they have chosen us to celebrate with them through birthdays, graduations, weddings, and many other special moments. We welcome you to look through our wide selection of cake toppers, indulge in our signature fresh fruit and whipped cream fillings, and enjoy custom decorated cakes for any occasion.
